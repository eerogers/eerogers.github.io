Elizabeth Rogers

Elizabeth has earned three master's degrees in addition to her B.A.

in International Studies, which she received from the University of Nebraska at Omaha.

Her first M.A. is in European and Russian Studies, from Yale

University; her second is in Sociocultural Anthropology, from Columbia University. At the end of 2020, she received her first M.S., in Cybersecurity, from Bellevue University.

Previous to entering the Cybersecurity program, she completed a

Coding Bootcamp at the University of California - Irvine; recently, in 2021, she completed the Data Science Career Track with Springboard. She's been studying to create projects that combine data analysis, website development and linguistic skills, utilizing her Russian, French, German, Modern Standard Arabic, and Japanese foreign language abilities; ultimately, she plans to create games that aid in foreign language acquisition and tools to aid in the processing of text data, with a general eye on cybersecurity application.

Elizabeth is skilled with Python, SQL, HTML, Javascript, jQuery and

CSS; she also has experience using MySQL, MongoDb, Firebase, and many Python libraries including Pandas, NumPy, Beautiful Soup, spaCy, NLTK, Matplotlib and Seaborn.
